Personal Meetings

In a cafe, restaurant or with you. A joint meeting in advance is a good and meaningful opportunity to get to know each other personally. You will learn more about my work and we can discuss all the details of your celebration, so that in the end, every moment of your wedding day shines in the right light.

Couple Shoot

We are happy to meet before the actual wedding. We can do the wedding location together or shoot some photos at a location of your choice. So you can get used to the camera in advance, I learn picture by picture more about your personal preferences and you can then directly use the resulting photos for the invitations.

Weddings

On the day of the wedding, I accompany you all day. Not only do I document the ceremony, but also put you and your loved ones in the scene from the beginning of the great day until the lavish party in the evening.

Post-Processing

After the wedding, the work is not done for me. Sighting, selection and editing of the images are part of the craft. You get access to two password-protected online galleries where your and your guests have access to the images. Of course, you get all the photos on USB stick and also in print version / as prints.

Fotobox

The photo box is always a lot of fun on every celebration. Your guests can shoot photos of themselves and get them directly on the touch screen, which is built into the box. If you want, you can dress up from a box of props with beards, wigs, etc. and the laughter is guaranteed.The photo box you can, if you like, add extra. You take the price on request from my price list.
